Aim:

Two ocellaris clownfish, Marlin and his wife Coral, admire their new home in the Great Barrier Reef and their
clutch of eggs when a barracuda attacks. All the eggs have been eaten by the barracuda. Marlin names this last
egg NEMO, a name that Coral liked.

Nemo develops a smaller right fin as a result of damage to his egg during the attack, which limits his
swimming ability. Worried about Nemo's safety, Marlin embarrasses Nemo during a school field trip. Nemo
sneaks away from the reef and is captured by scuba divers.

While attempting to save Nemo, Marlin meets YOU, now only you can save NEMO by finding correct path by
your amazing eyes, so here is you're CHALLENGE

You have to make an autonomous bot which will decide its path using image processing and it would finally
find NEMO.

Team Specification:

A team can consist of maximum of 4 participants. Every team should have a unique name.

Bot Specifications:

  • The dimensions of the bot should not be exceeding (50cm)*(50cm)*(50cm).
  • The weight of the bot should not exceed 20 kg, including all batteries, laptop sensors, and on-board
    circuits, etc.
  • The bot must be autonomous.

Rules:

  • You can use your mobile and laptop as well but you can't change command after you have started
    your journey i.e. after the bot is started.
  • The bot should take real time images and act accordingly.
  • It has to identify colors and depending on color orientation it has to decide its path. Always bot has to
    turn in the direction of green color. Say green is on left and red is on right then it has to turn left side.
  • The balls which we are going to use in the competition will be given to the competitors few days
    before the competition. And distance from the ball at which the bot has to make the turn also will be
    told to them.
  • After taking correct paths it has to finally reach NEMO and then by identifying a different
    color(NEMO) blue, a song must be played depending on your choice like "KOI MIL GAYA".
  • Every team will be given maximum 3 chances.
  • For every wrong turn penalty will be given.

Arena:

This is the sample arena it will be changed during the competition to avoid discrepancies.

Scoring Criteria:

  • For every correct turn 30 points will be awarded and for every wrong turn a penalty of 10
    points will be given.
  • For finding Nemo 50 points will be awarded and after the song is played 20 more points
    will be awarded.
  • In case of tie timing will be considered the tie breaking factor.
  • In case of any discrepancies the organizer's decision would be the final decision.

Safety Rules:

  • Special care should be taken to protect the on-board batteries; bot without proper protection
    will not be allowed to compete.
  • If you have a bot that does not fit within the categories set forth in these rules or is in some
    way ambiguous or borderline, please contact the event organizers.
  • Safe innovation is always encouraged, but surprising the organizers with your brilliant
    exploitation of a loophole may cause your robot to be disqualified before it even competes.
