INTRODUCTION
This style consists of a two-on-two debate, between the affirmative team, known as the Government or the Proposition, and the negative team, referred to as the Opposition.
Rules:
1. A debate round has two teams with two debaters each and a Speaker.
2. The Speaker serves as both the judge and arbiter of the rules during the round.
3. Note here that "Speaker" always refers to the judge from this point forward.
4. One team represents the Government, while the other represents the Opposition.
