Objective

Objective of this event is to find the solution to the following problems or give detailed information about the solution other countries have.
  1. Reuse of water
  2. Internet and mobile technology to all
  3. Using garbage to solve some other problem.
  4. Open topic.

Problem Statement

    Your team need to present an idea of a working model with PPT or a physical model to solve any of the following problem :-
  1. It has been presumed by many that third world war will happen due to the outsourcing of water. We will for sure need a plan in future to reuse the wastewater more effectively since we are not right now.
  2. Though government is providing many apps and data over internet to help farmers on having a better yield. It has been seen that plenty of farmers are not able to take full benefit from the schemes primarily due to the lack of internet or mobile technology. Google and Facebook are also trying hard to provide internet to all.
  3. Scotland have found a way to convert their garbage into usable energy. It is so effect that they are now importing garbage from their neighbour countries. We (Indians), on the other hand, are suffering to implement Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and at the same time are not able to fulfill our energy problems.
  4. Open topic is accepted as long as it solves any major problem. The teams have to report the topic to the organisers before hand.

Rules :

  1. Language of presentation could be either Hindi or English.
  2. A team can consist of maximum of 3 people.
  3. Maximum of 10 minutes will be given to a team to present their idea. Judges may ask questions from the team after their presentation. Be prepared for it.

General Guidelines :

  1. Choose a topic.
  2. Think of a solution.
  3. Present your solution through a Powerpoint presentation or a physical model. If using Powerpoint , include pictures of the model.
  4. If through a Powerpoint presentation, a copy of it will be kept by us. If through a physical model, it will be kept by us till the results are declared.
  5. The intellectual property of model/idea will reside with the participants before and after the end of the event, weather the team wins or not.

Judging Criteria :

  1. How much feasible the model is in real life.
  2. Explanation of the revenue model with the scale of the model.
  3. Rough estimation and facts and analysis to support the idea.
  4. Explanation of the concept, confidence, presentation.
